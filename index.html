Safari Simulator

A 2D simulator game which I created together with two fellow students as a university project. The aim of the game is to ensure as many different animals are present on the safari as possible, attracting more tourists, which in turn earns you money.

Railways

A simple web based game in which you must place rails to connect all of the tiles. Left clicking on a tile cycles through different rail orientations and a right click removes the rail altogether. On completion, a leaderboard appears containing all finishing times .
